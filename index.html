Frequently Asked Questions

What features are included in each plan?

Each plan offers a set of features tailored to different business needs. The Basic Plan includes essential accounting and inventory tools. The Professional Plan adds advanced reporting, billing, and multi-user access. The Enterprise Plan provides all features of the Professional Plan, plus advanced forecasting, custom integrations, and dedicated support.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can easily upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Simply contact our support team or use the account management portal to adjust your subscription based on your changing needs.

How secure is my data with ProzFlex?

We prioritize data security and use advanced encryption and security measures to protect your information. Regular backups and compliance with industry standards ensure your data is safe and secure.

What kind of customer support do you offer?

Our support varies by plan. Basic Plan users receive standard support, while Professional Plan users get priority support. Enterprise Plan users benefit from 24/7 customer support and a dedicated account manager to assist with any issues.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a free trial for new users to experience our software and explore its features. The trial period allows you to test the functionalities and see how ProzFlex fits your business needs before committing to a subscription.
